Instead of the traditional view of a typeface as a collection of static styles, GT Flexa embraces the idea of a fluid design space. As a dynamic tool, it enables joyful typesetting that allows for fully responsive designs. Its creators, Dominik & Marc, spent six years fine-tuning GT Flexa’s concept and character. The result is an impressively extensive typographic system with a unique personality. We’re proud to publish this beautiful piece of design and prove to you that a grotesk typeface can still be fun.
